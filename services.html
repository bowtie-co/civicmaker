Each step in this sought after process ensures a smooth and robust experience as your website moves from whiteboard to launch.

  • Review of vision, goals, structure, and content
  • Detailed needs assessment and development of user stories

Conceptual + Primary Design

Development

Usability Testing

Launch + Training

  • Customized training documentation and quickstart guides

Piece of Mind

Hosting + Maintenance

Hosting, maintenance, and backups should never be a concern. And with CivicMaker, we have taken specific and significant steps to ensure that they never are. Hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) for industry leading hosting architecture for maximum uptime and redundancy means instant scalability, built in protection from attacks like DDOS attempts, automatic systems failovers, updates without service interruption, and so much more.

Accessibility

CivicMaker believes everyone should have access to your website. For this reason, we follow Section 508 and WCAG 2.0 compliance guidelines. Never wonder if you are supporting everyone that wants to view your site--with CivicMaker you already are.

Analytics

CivicMaker knows that measuring success is important. With that in mind, your CivicMaker site comes pre-wired and set with all the analytics tools you would need to understand and proactively engage your audience
