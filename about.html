About Us

Over the past 20 years, we’ve partnered with over 2,300 local government clients, helping them connect with citizens in more meaningful ways. With our CivicEngage, CivicHR and CivicReady software suites, we tailor our easy-to-use solutions to meet your unique needs. And while we have powerful programs and intuitive technology, it’s our people who truly make it all work for local government.

Our expert team partners with you to develop a clear vision of success. We take a step back, ask a lot of questions and gain clarity around the issues before we do anything else. With a true understanding of your needs, we then align our solutions and make sure that what we’re doing is not only integrated, but actionable, too.
